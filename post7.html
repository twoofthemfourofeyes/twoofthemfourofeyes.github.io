interaction sounds of a bop-it extreme

users interact with a bop-it extreme through pressing the bop-it button (bopping it), flicking the green flicking sensor, pulling the blue pull tab, twisting the yellow twist sensor and sinning the red spin wheel.

the context of the aforementioned interactions in my experience was between the years of 2002 - 2009. i familiarized myself with the interactions when i was by myself on weekends and early evenings after school.
